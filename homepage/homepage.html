How does it work?









Getting your key

You’ll be able to communicate directly with your host to find out how to get your keys. Whether you meet them personally or simply use a lockbox, it’ll be easy for you to 
access your property and start enjoying your holiday!





Communicating with your host

You can always contact your host with any questions leading up to your trip. Perhaps you want to let them know what time you’re arriving or you have a special request – enjoy stress-free communication at all times.





Checking in

Maybe you’ve always stayed in hotels and you’re not sure how to ‘check-in’ to an apartment or holiday home. Don’t worry, other guests have felt the same! That’s 
why hosts provide all the information you need to get you settled in.
